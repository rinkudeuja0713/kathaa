Safe & Confidential

You deserve real support

These are trusted organizations in Nepal that work specifically with survivors of gender-based violence and harassment. All of them are confidential. None of them will blame you.

Provides counseling, temporary shelter, and support specifically for survivors of gender-based violence and domestic abuse. Safe, confidential, and survivor-centered.

Free legal consultation and representation for women. They handle cases of sexual harassment, assault, and workplace discrimination under Nepal's Sexual Harassment at Workplace Prevention Act 2014.

The government body responsible for protecting women's rights in Nepal. Can receive complaints, conduct investigations, and refer cases to relevant authorities.

Works to rescue and rehabilitate survivors of trafficking and sexual exploitation. Provides shelter, counseling, legal aid, and vocational training for survivors.
📞 01-4479142
🌐 maitinepal.org
You do not have to file a case to get support. All of the organizations above offer counseling and help without requiring you to report to police. Reporting is your choice, at your pace, if and when you are ready. What happened to you was not your fault — and you deserve support regardless of what you decide to do next.
